Our Story

Sirisena Jewellery Workshop

Since 2021, Sirisena Jewellers has been guiding artisans to explore stunning designs and craft breathtaking pieces. From simple pendants to intricate sets, we craft experiences that inspire and excite.

Discover exquisite handcrafted jewellery and learn the art of jewellery making at Sirisena Jewellery Workshop. Explore our latest collections and workshop schedules.

Turn your vision into reality with our custom design service. Our expert jewelers will work with you to create a unique piece that reflects your personal style and story. From concept to completion, we'll bring your dream jewelry to life.
Bring your damaged or worn jewelry back to its former glory with our expert repair services. From simple fixes to complex restorations, our skilled jewelers will handle your precious pieces with care. Trust us to make your jewelry shine again.
Whether you've inherited a loose gemstone, want to redesign an existing piece, or are creating a custom design, our gemstone setting service is here to meet your needs. We'll work closely with you to select the perfect setting style that complements your stone and personal style. Our commitment to quality ensures your gemstone is securely and beautifully set, ready to be treasured for generations.
